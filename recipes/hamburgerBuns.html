Hamburger Buns

Hamburger Buns

Nutrition facts per Serving: 295 calories; protein 8.9g; carbohydrates 48.8g; fat 8.3g; sodium 144.7mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • ¼ cup margarine, melted
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 6 cups all-purpose flour, or as needed

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, stir together the milk, margarine, warm water, sugar and yeast.
  2. Let stand for about 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in the salt, and gradually stir in the flour until you have a soft dough.
  4. Divide into 25 pieces, and form into balls. Place on baking sheets so they are 2 to 3 inches apart.
    Let rise for 20 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Bake the rolls for 15 minutes in the preheated oven.
    Cool slightly, then split them in half horizontally to fill with your favorite burgers.
